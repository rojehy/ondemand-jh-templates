On-Demand Rules of Behavior

  1. Compute system(s) for which you have been issued an account may only be used for official Science Team activities on the on-demand system for the Mission.
  2. Users are expected to terminate all notebooks, terminals and processes, as well as shutdown their server instances when they are no longer needed and prior to logging out. (Note that notebooks are not terminated by logging out alone.)
  3. Idle notebooks will be culled after 14 days.
  4. All software on the IT resource is protected in accordance with NASA and Federal Government security and control procedures which will be adhered to.
  5. Use of these IT resources gives consent for monitoring and security testing to ensure proper security procedures and appropriate usage are being observed.
  6. IT resources will not be used for fraudulent, harassing or obscene messages and/or materials.
  7. Tampering with another user's account, files, data or processes without the other user's express permission, use of the system resources for personal purposes, or other unauthorized activities is strictly prohibited and will result in termination of access privileges.
  8. Logon ID's, passwords, and passphrases may never be transferred or shared for any reason.
  9. Passwords:
    1. will be a minimum of 8 alphanumeric characters;
    2. will be memorized and not written down;
    3. will not be stored in keyboard macros, script, or batch files;
    4. will not consist of personal ID data or be easily guessable;
  10. When access is no longer required to these IT resources, notify appropriate responsible parties and make no further attempt to access these resources.
  11. Failure to adhere to these rules or subvert these rules may constitute grounds for termination of access privileges, administrative action, and/or civil or criminal prosecution.
